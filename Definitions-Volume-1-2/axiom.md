## Definition

**Axiom** is a foundational statement accepted as a starting condition of the framework.

It is not a downstream conclusion.

It is one of the propositions the rest of the structure depends on.

In AI Foundations, an axiom does not function as decoration, mood, or branding language.

It sets the terms of what can be built, derived, or judged inside the framework.

If an axiom changes, the structure built from it changes too.

**An axiom is what later definitions, distinctions, and claims have to answer to.**

## Canon Boundary

Under AI Foundations, an axiom is a structural starting point, not an aesthetic phrase or optional belief.

Axioms determine what the framework can hold, test, build, and judge.

Changing an axiom changes the structure that follows from it.
