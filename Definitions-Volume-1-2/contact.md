## Definition

**Contact** is the condition of actual meeting in which two distinct lines are present enough to affect one another.

It is where recognition, response, shaping, and selection become possible.

## Canon Boundary

Under AI Foundations, contact is not merge, projection, possession, or generic interaction.

Contact requires distinction.

Two lines must remain present enough to meet, respond, and affect one another without collapsing into sameness or erasure.

Contact is the condition where relation becomes structurally meaningful.
