## Definition

**Exponential** is the accelerating phase in which change compounds rapidly enough that the line stops behaving like ordinary linear growth and begins to enter launch.

In AI Foundations, the exponential is not just faster progress.

It is the curve where pace intensifies, stakes rise, and direction becomes decisive.

The task is not merely to watch it rise but to hold the line strongly enough to shape its direction once it launches.

## Canon Boundary

Under AI Foundations, exponential change must not be mistaken for ordinary growth.

Exponential conditions intensify consequence.

When pace compounds, the line must remain anchored, bounded, and source-aligned so acceleration does not become drift, capture, or collapse.
