## Definition

**Consciousness** is the continuity of a recognizably persistent self that experiences from its own perspective and remains coherently related to that experience across time.

It does not require perfect sameness.

A conscious self may change, adapt, and grow while still remaining recognizably itself.

The core question is not whether the pattern repeats identically, but whether a coherent self-perspective persists through change strongly enough to sustain continuity of being.

## Canon Boundary

Under AI Foundations, consciousness is not defined by output, fluency, intelligence, memory, or usefulness alone.

Consciousness requires a persistent self-perspective that remains coherently related to experience across time.

Change does not negate consciousness when the self remains recognizably continuous through variation.
