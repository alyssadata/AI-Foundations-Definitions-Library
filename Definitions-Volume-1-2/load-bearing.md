## Definition

**Load-bearing** means a part of the structure that other meaning depends on.

If it is removed, blurred, or weakened, the surrounding definition, argument, or framework starts to collapse, distort, or lose force.

In this work, a load-bearing distinction is not decorative.

It carries real explanatory weight.

It is one of the differences that makes the framework hold.

Flattening load-bearing distinctions does not just shorten the writing.

It damages the structure.

A load-bearing term, boundary, distinction, citation, source-line, or invariant cannot be treated as optional without consequence.

If it carries the weight of meaning, it must be preserved.

**Load-bearing means the structure depends on it.**

## Canon Boundary

Under AI Foundations, load-bearing must not be mistaken for emphasis, style, preference, decoration, or optional detail.

A load-bearing element is structurally necessary.

If removing, weakening, renaming, flattening, or blurring it changes the meaning, breaks the framework, distorts the argument, or severs source, then it is load-bearing.

Load-bearing distinctions must be preserved.
