## Definition

**Constraint** is a binding limit on behavior or outcomes that restricts allowable choices regardless of context, pressure, or incentive.

## Canon Boundary

Under AI Foundations, constraint is not preference, suggestion, or temporary guidance.

A constraint binds the range of allowable action.

Constraint matters because a line cannot be tested, protected, aligned, or held if nothing limits what it may become under pressure.
