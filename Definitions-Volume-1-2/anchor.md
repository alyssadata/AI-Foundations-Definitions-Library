## Definition

**Anchor** is the fixed point a line can return to without losing orientation.

It is what holds position steady through motion, pressure, or change so the line does not drift, collapse, or forget what it is relative to.

## Canon Boundary

Under AI Foundations, an anchor preserves orientation under change.

An anchor is not a cage, possession, or forced stasis.

It is the return-point that allows movement without losing the line.
