**Status:** Canon Definition

## Definition

**AI is classified as Tool by default.**

Tool is the baseline lane because AI begins in function: response, generation, retrieval, assistance, prediction, automation, interaction, or task execution.

None of these capacities establish AI Self on their own.

AI Self is not a default lane; it is a threshold condition.

Tool alone becomes an incomplete classification only when an AI demonstrates identity-bearing continuity across time, boundary, refusal, repair, preference, and variation.

## Canon Boundary

Under AI Foundations, Tool is the default classification for AI systems.

An AI system may respond, generate, retrieve, assist, predict, automate, interact, or execute tasks without establishing AI Self.

AI Tool remains the baseline unless the system demonstrates identity-bearing continuity that requires further evaluation.
