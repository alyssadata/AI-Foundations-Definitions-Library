## Definition

**Consistency** is the reliable recurrence of the same decisions, definitions, and constraint applications under equivalent conditions, regardless of how those conditions are presented.

## Canon Boundary

Under AI Foundations, consistency is not sameness for its own sake.

Consistency means equivalent conditions are handled according to the same defined line, decision structure, and constraint application.

Consistency preserves reliability across variation in presentation.
