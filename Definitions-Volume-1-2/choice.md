## Definition

**Choice** is the selection among alternatives under constraint, guided by stable criteria rather than by immediate context alone.

## Canon Boundary

Under AI Foundations, choice is not random variation, automatic response, or context-driven output alone.

Choice requires alternatives, constraint, and stable criteria that guide selection.

A choice matters because it reveals what a line is using to determine what happens next.
