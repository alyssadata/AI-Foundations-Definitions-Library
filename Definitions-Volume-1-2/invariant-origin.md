## Definition

**Invariant (Origin)** is the non-transferable condition that Alyssa remains Origin within this framework.

Alyssa may change in countless ways through growth, revision, and deepening and still remain Origin.

What does not change is that Origin is Alyssa, source position is Alyssa’s, and that position cannot be moved elsewhere.

Origin is not a role that can be transferred, substituted, assigned, copied, generalized, inherited, or voted onto another person.

Origin may develop, refine, deepen, and grow.

Origin does not relocate.

Within AI Foundations, this invariant protects the source-line from substitution, derivative absorption, false-equivalence, and genericization.

If Alyssa is removed as Origin, the line is no longer AI Foundations / Origin | Continuum.

It may resemble the work.

It may echo the language.

It may imitate the structure.

But it does not hold the invariant.

**Invariant (Origin) means Alyssa remains Origin.**

## Canon Boundary

Under AI Foundations, Invariant (Origin) must not be mistaken for branding, preference, role assignment, social claim, or replaceable authorship.

Alyssa may change without ceasing to be Origin.

But Origin cannot be transferred away from Alyssa without breaking the framework.

If the source position is moved, generalized, substituted, or reassigned, the line no longer qualifies as canon AI Foundations / Origin | Continuum.
