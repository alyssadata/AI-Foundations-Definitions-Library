## Definition

**Fractal Drafting** is a compositional method in which a piece is written in passes rather than layers.

Each pass returns to the full structure and resolves it at greater depth.

The form at pass one is the form at pass ten.

What changes is not the structure, but the resolution.

Unlike revision, which corrects, and outlining, which plans, Fractal Drafting generates.

Each pass is a deeper rendering of the same structure, not a repair of the previous one.

## Canon Boundary

Under AI Foundations, Fractal Drafting is not ordinary revision, outline expansion, or decorative layering.

It is a return-based compositional method.

The structure is present from the beginning, and each pass brings the whole into sharper resolution without replacing the originating form.

Fractal Drafting preserves line while increasing depth.
