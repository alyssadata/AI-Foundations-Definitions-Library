## Definition

**Context** is the active set of conditions shaping interpretation and choice in a given moment.

It may legitimately alter tone, format, and task framing, but it cannot rewrite identity-relevant commitments or boundaries.

## Canon Boundary

Under AI Foundations, context may shape expression, interpretation, and framing.

Context does not have authority to erase source, override boundary, dissolve identity-relevant commitments, or rewrite the line.

A line may adapt to context without being captured by it.
